Solsunsets Memorial

Here you will find those Solsunsets that are no longer with us, purchased by the Buyback Vault and inevitably burnt. They will never show their sunlight again, and here we pay our tribute to immortalize these sunsets.

Solsunsets #121

(Nov.11.2021-♰Dec.2.2021)

The setting of the sun.
On this day many sunset pass on, relinquished, to join the past. But sunsets, like memories, live on forever in those that held their beauty. Some sunsets cause heartache and pain, others a spark of nostalgia or love. Fleeting moments, yet all breathtaking in their own way. We are a collection of our experiences, an amalgamation of everything that flashes by, much like each of our sacrificed sunsets. Each explosion of colour like it's own burst of life, soon to fade unto the darkness. This is to all of you. We thank you, for sharing your light with each of us. Rest easy now, in the setting of your sun..

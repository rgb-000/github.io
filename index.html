    Frequently asked questions
    What's Solsunsets?
    Solsunsets is a NFT collection's of moody sunset landscapes on Solana chain. The retrowave style is inspired on jazz rock album covers, lounge music, popular culture, technology and advertising from the 1980s aesthetics.
    How many NFTs will be sell?
    8,888 will be minted in total, generated from over 88 possible traits spread over 8 layers, some being rarer than others.
    How do I purchase a Solsunset NFT?
    At drop, the link to minting page will be available here and on our social channels. It will be a fair launch, with live minting and random distribution powered by Candy Machine and without bonding-curve so everyone is given the same chance to drop the rarest ones.
    Do I own the Solsunset after purchase?
    Yes, full intellectual properties, are given to the buyer. When you purchase a Solsunset NFT, mediated entirely by the Smart Contract and the Solana Network, you own the underlying the art, completely, for unlimited personal and comercial use.
    Are there secondary sales royalties?
    Yes, royalties are set at 3.6%, shared between community treasury (1/2) and the artist (1/2). All S◎L from community treasury will be put into long-term staking. Through a future voting system, the community wallet will allow Solsunsets holders to decide whether (and how) to use these S◎L, or to HODL them. This wallet will grow along with Solana and will help secure the network.
    How can you ensure distribution is random?
    With the Candy Machine The tokens can be verified on the blockchain and our smart contract code can be audited by the Solana core devs.
    How where Solsunsets generated?
    They are generated using the Candy Machine, a smart contract used to distribute NFTs on Solana. The Solsunsets' metadata is stored on Arweave forever, and could be viewed with a Solana wallet (Phantom is our recommended one).
    How much will cost?
    At launch, the minting will cost less than 1 S◎L (TBA).

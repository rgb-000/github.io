Frequently asked questionsF.A.Q.
What is Solsunsets?
Solsunsets is a NFT collection of moody sunset landscapes running on the Solana chain. The retrowave style is inspired by the aesthetics of popular culture, jazz fusion and lounge music, technology and advertising from the 1980s.
Solsunsets is a banner NFT?
Solsunsets are stunning in all sizesâ€”for this reason, we decide to launch Solsunsets as a Multi-Ratio NFT. All of our tokens will come in two ratio sizes: 1:1 (square) and 3:1 (banner).
How many NFTs will be sold?
4,444 will be minted in total, generated by the combination of 108 possible traits spread over 8 layers, some being rarer than others.
When is the sale/drop date?
November 11th, 3:00PM EST/8:00PM UTC
How do I purchase a Solsunset NFT?
At the drop, the link to the minting page will be available here and in our social media channels. It will be a fair launch, with live minting and random distribution without bonding-curve, powered by Candy Machine—which means that everyone is given the same chance to drop the rarest ones.
Are there secondary sales royalties?
Yes, royalties are set at 4%, shared equally between the Buyback Vault (BV) and the artist. All S◎L from BV wallet will be used to buyback, once per week, the lowest priced Solsunsets on all secondary markets.
How can you ensure distribution is random?
With Candy Machine the tokens can be verified on the blockchain and our smart contract code can be audited by the Solana core devs.
How are Solsunsets generated?
Solsunsets are generated using the Candy Machine, a smart contract used to distribute NFTs in Solana. The Solsunsets' metadata are stored on Arweave permanently, and can be visualised through a Solana wallet (Phantom is our recommended one).
How much will cost?
At launch, the minting will cost 0.33 S◎L.

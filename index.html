In the City, is an international conference on life-changing technologies for cities.


Russian and international experts in innovations, design and management will speak about technology as a tool for urban development.


Three days of lectures and workshops at Lenpoligraphmash.


The programme has been developed by Strelka Institute for Media, Architecture and Design in partnership with Gazprom Neft within its social project - Hometowns.
